<b>Requirements (Instruments, Chemicals & Other) : </b><br>
•	Centrifugal pump<br>
•	Volumetric tank<br>
•	Stop watch<br>
•	Pressure gauge<br>
•	Thermometer<br>
•	Energy meter<br>
•	Gate valve<br><br>

<b>Procedure : </b><br>
•	Clean the apparatus and make all tanks free from dust<br> 
•	Close the drain valve provided<br> 
•	Open flow control valve given on the water discharge<br> 
•	Now switch on the main power supply <br>
•	Operate the flow control valve to regulate the flow of water<br>
•	Set the desired RPM of motor/ Pump <br>
•	Operate the control valve to regulate the suction of pump <br>
•	Record discharge pressure by means of pressure gauge <br>
•	Record suction pressure by means of suction gauge.<br> 
•	Measure the discharge by measuring tank by using stop watch. <br>
•	Repeat the same procedure for different speed.<br>
